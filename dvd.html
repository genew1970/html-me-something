Rogue One

Rogue One is an action-packed movie for all ages. In my opinion, Rogue One ranks just below Empire Strikes Back (number one) in my personal ranking. The story does have a few slow spots. Rogue One is entertaining and should hit the stores on DVD/Blu-ray April 4th. It will be available digitally March 24th.
Premise: A band of mercenaries set out to steal the plans to the Death Star.
Pros: Felicity Jones is phenomenal in Rogue One. Donnie Yen brings martial arts to the Star Wars universe. K2SO is a great robot companion! I want one!
Cons: The slow parts of the movie break up the action sequences in a reasonable manner. Some scenes were a bit too slow. Cassian could have used some back story to help identify with the character.

Final verdict: 4.5/5


Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them

Fantastic Beasts is a movie spawned from the Harry Potter universe which provides entertainment throughout the movie. The movie is light-hearted with plenty of laughs for kids and adults alike. My wife has not seen a single Harry Potter movie and did not require explanations. This is good news for those individuals who have not seen the movies.
Premise: The main character is tracking down some lost beasts as the characters explore a wonderful world of fantasy.
Pros: The movie provides lots of laughs and is easy to follow. Eddie Redmayne provides a brilliant performance as the lead character.
Cons: It seemed some characters needed a bit more explanation.

Final verdict: 4.5/5
